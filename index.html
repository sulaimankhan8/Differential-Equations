Taylor Series Method

The Taylor series method is a numerical technique used to solve ordinary differential equations (ODEs). This method approximates the solution of an ODE by expanding it into a Taylor series around a point. The Taylor series method is particularly useful for solving initial value problems.

Taylor Series Method for solving ODEs

consider an initial value problem

y'(x)= f(x,y),   y(x0)=y0

The Taylor series expansion of the solution y(x) around x0 is given by:

y(x) = y(x0) + (x − x0)y'(x0) + (x-x0)2 2!y"(x0) + (x-x0)3 3!y'''(x0) + . . .
